About Us

About Us

I'm a cat lover just like you. So when there is something wrong with one of my animals I freak out. One day I heard a noise and found my cat (Mr. Cheerio) behind the couch breathing very hard with his poop all around him. We took him to the emergency room and they ran test after test, but couldn't find anything wrong with him. A couple weeks go by and he's acting normal. In the middle of the night our cat wakes my wife up to give her loves and all of a sudden falls over, starts paddling his feet, and starts shaking to the point of defecating himself. We have never seen a seizure before so this frightened us dearly. We took our cat back in and they did more blood work and x-rays but still could not find anything. The doctor told us to wait before giving him medicine because sometimes the seizures will go away on it's own.

A couple weeks went by with more seizures but finally they stopped. We aren't sure why they happened in the first place. While this was going on we did much research into seizures with cats. Alot of the articles that we read were hard to read, or we just couldn't find the information that we were looking for. I decided that I wanted to build a website on cat seizures that was easy on the eyes, and easy to find the information you are looking for. Without having to search multiple websites.

Clearly Cheerio does not want me too write anymore.
